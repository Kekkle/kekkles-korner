# About Kekkle me pink

Hey there! I'm Joykie, the person behind **Kekkle**.

I'm building a nutrition education app designed for kids aged 10--12. The goal is to help young people understand how the food they eat actually powers their bodies --- without any of the guilt, shame, or confusion that often comes with nutrition talk.

This blog is where I share bi-monthly updates on how the project is going: what I'm building, what I'm learning, what's tricky, and what's exciting.

## What's the app about?

The app teaches kids about nutrients --- carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals --- through playful analogies, friendly characters, and interactive games. Think of it as a curious guide that walks *with* kids through the science of food, not a textbook that talks *at* them.

There's no "good food / bad food" language. No calorie counting. Just real, empowering knowledge that helps kids make sense of what they eat and why it matters.

## Why a blog?

Building something from scratch is a journey, and I want to document it --- the wins, the stumbles, the design decisions, and the moments where things finally click. If you're interested in ed-tech, nutrition education, or just enjoy following along with a project as it grows, you're in the right place.

Thanks for stopping by!
