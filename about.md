# About Me

I'm Joykie Walter, a Nutrition Science graduate and Nutrition Content developer. Originally from Cape Town, South Africa, I now live in southern Germany with my German Mann and three German Kinder, and this beautiful yet-not-always-warm-enough-place has very much become home to me.

## How I got here

I had spent close to a decade working in publishing, where I fell in love with the process of turning ideas into polished, well-researched content. Technical writing, editorial development, research, graphic design, even illustrations--I got to do a bit of everything, and it taught me how to take complicated topics and make them accessible no matter the audience.

But the transition to the field of nutrition started from a personal place. As a teenager, I struggled with an eating disorder, and honestly, I had no real understanding of what proper nutrition meant, especially for someone who also wanted to be active and build muscle. That gap in knowledge stuck with me, and eventually, curiosity turned into purpose, and in 2020 I went back to study, earning a BSc in Nutritional Science.

## What I care about

These days, I'm passionate about helping young people build a healthy, grounded relationship with food, one that's focused on nourishment and supporting what their bodies actually need to thrive. I'm also interested in the use of gamification as a learning tool. There's something about interactive, playful formats that helps tricky concepts click and actually stick. That's a lot of what I'm exploring on this blog: building gamified nutrition content, especially for younger audiences who deserve accurate information in accessible formats.

## When I'm not working

You'll probably find me training for my first Hyrox (send good vibes), watching reels with my three teens, beating my husband at backgammon, sending a podcast-level voicenote to my bestie, or just sipping a creamy coffee at the kitchen window. :)

Thanks for stopping by — I hope you find something here that's useful, interesting, or at least mildly entertaining.
